Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for a complaint to be resolved?

High urgency complaints (like water leaks) are typically addressed within 2-4 hours. Medium and Low urgency issues are resolved within 24-48 hours depending on staff availability.

Can I edit a complaint after submitting?

Currently, you cannot edit a complaint once submitted. If you made a mistake, please contact the admin directly or submit a new complaint and mention the previous one.

What if an issue is marked "Resolved" but it is not?

Please immediately inform your Hostel Warden or submit a follow-up complaint explicitly mentioning that the previous resolution was unsatisfactory.
